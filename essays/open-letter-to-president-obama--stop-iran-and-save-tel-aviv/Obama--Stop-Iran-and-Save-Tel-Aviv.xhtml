TO: Mr. Barack Obama, President of the United States of America.

FROM: Mr. Shlomi Fish: Tel Aviv, Israel-based humorist, essayist, Internet entertainer, amateur philosopher, polymath/Renaissance man, and superb geek / hacker / action hero / warrior.

ABSTRACT: stop the Iranian Leadership from developing a nuclear bomb, and immediately take decisive action (detailed below) to neutralise it, and overthrow it, in order to prevent the destruction of Tel Aviv, Israel and/or some other big Middle Eastern centre of life, value production, and happiness.

Dear Mr. Obama,

What would you rather have? One country's oil being gone forever, or an atomic bomb being dropped on New York City, Los Angeles, or San Francisco and wiping one of them forever? The Jewish scholars once said that “He who saved one soul, has saved the world entire” and saving an entire city or country of happy, productive, people is much more important and a humane cause than gold, even the black one.
